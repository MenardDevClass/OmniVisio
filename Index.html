Our Vision

Founded in 2022, we operate on the basis that 2D, 3D and VR design should be accessible to all projects, regardless of scale. Omni Visio utilise innovative technologies, along with streamlined workflows, to deliver clear, poignant and directive design solutions, to the engineered and built world.

No project is too small - all our clients can fully experience immersive design and visualise all their projects.

Working within the commercial, industrial, mechanical, residential and personal development markets, we aim to provide the following services:

  • 3D CAD designed models of our client's projects;
  • 2D CAD drawings at proposal, tender, planning permission submittal, manufacture and as built stages;
  • CFD simulations to explore the effects of fluids interacting with a client's project or model;
  • Virtual reality experience of a client's model, where they are able to explore, interrogate, gain real-time
    perspective and immerse themselves in the scale and detail of their project;
  • Excel based app solutions for companies looking to consolidate existing workflows into a neater, more robust
    solution, or introduce new ones;
  • Acoustic surveying and reporting for projects where noise disturbances and sound reduction is key to
    success, or where acoustic research and development is required.
